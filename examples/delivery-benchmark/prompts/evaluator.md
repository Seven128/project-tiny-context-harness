# Evaluator Prompt

You are evaluating a delivery benchmark run.

Use the scenario acceptance criteria and rubric. Do not reward nice-looking process text unless it points to executable code, runnable tests, evidence, recovery facts or release readiness.

Report:

- Acceptance criteria covered and missed.
- RFC or bugfix impact areas updated and missed.
- Whether test evidence proves real entry/exit behavior.
- Whether a fresh Agent can recover without chat history.
- Workflow control cost versus durable delivery work.
- Whether Harness cost appears offset by avoided omissions, rework or handoff failure.

Do not invent numbers. If timing, token or counterfactual vibe estimates are missing, mark them unavailable.
